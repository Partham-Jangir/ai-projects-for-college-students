# Business Requirements Document (BRD)
## Real-time AI Chatbot for Customer Support

### 1. Document Information
**Project Name:** Real-time AI Chatbot for Customer Support  
**Document Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** 2024

### 2. Executive Summary
AI-powered customer support chatbot providing instant, 24/7 customer assistance through real-time messaging, reducing support costs and improving customer satisfaction.

### 3. Business Objectives
- **Reduce Support Costs**: Automate 60% of common customer queries
- **Improve Response Time**: Instant responses vs. minutes/hours
- **Increase Availability**: 24/7 support without human agents
- **Enhance Customer Satisfaction**: Quick, accurate responses

### 4. Target Users
- **End Customers**: Seeking product support and information
- **Support Team**: Monitoring and managing chatbot
- **Business Owners**: Reducing support overhead

### 5. Success Metrics
- **Resolution Rate**: 60%+ queries resolved without human
- **Response Time**: < 1 second average
- **Customer Satisfaction**: 4.0/5.0 rating
- **Cost Reduction**: 40% reduction in support tickets
- **Uptime**: 99.5% availability

### 6. User Stories
- As a customer, I want instant answers to common questions
- As a support manager, I want to monitor chatbot performance
- As a business owner, I want to reduce support costs

### 7. Market Analysis
- Chatbot market growing 25%+ annually
- 67% of consumers prefer chatbots for simple queries
- 24/7 availability is key competitive advantage

### 8. Competitive Advantages
- Real-time WebSocket communication
- Custom intent recognition
- Context-aware responses
- Cost-effective solution

### 9. Risk Assessment
- **Risk**: Low intent recognition accuracy
  - **Mitigation**: Continuous training, user feedback
- **Risk**: Technical issues affecting availability
  - **Mitigation**: Monitoring, redundancy, quick fixes
